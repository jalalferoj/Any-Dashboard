About This Dashboard

This application is a powerful, client-side data visualization tool that allows you to upload your own data from CSV or Excel files and instantly generate a variety of interactive charts. It's designed to be a quick and easy way to explore and understand your data without needing to install any software or rely on complex business intelligence platforms. All data processing happens directly in your browser, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

Key Features

  • Upload Your Data: Supports popular file formats like CSV, XLSX, and XLS. Your data is processed locally in your browser and is never uploaded to a server.
  • Versatile Charting: Create a wide range of charts, including vertical and horizontal bars, grouped and stacked bars, mixed bar-and-line charts, and more.
  • Interactive Controls: Dynamically change the chart type, select columns for X and Y axes, and group data by different categories to drill down into your insights.
  • Data Aggregation: Easily summarize your data by calculating the sum, average, or count of your values directly within the chart.
  • Modern Interface: Enjoy a clean, responsive design with both light and dark modes for your viewing comfort.
  • Export & Share: Download any chart as a high-quality PNG image to use in your reports, presentations, or publications.
